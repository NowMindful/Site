Privacy


At Now Mindful, your privacy matters to us. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your personal information.


1. Information We Collect

We collect only the following types of information:

  • Email Address: If you email us, we collect your email address for correspondence.
  • Analytics Data: Aggregated and anonymized data from Google Analytics to understand website usage and improve our services.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely for the following purposes:

  • Responding to your emails.
  • Analyzing site usage to enhance user experience.

3. Data Storage

Email correspondence is securely stored in our email system. Analytics data is securely processed and stored on Google's servers.


4. Marketing

We do not send marketing emails. You may see updates or promotions if you follow us on social media.


5. Cookies

We use cookies to enhance your browsing experience and for Google Analytics. Cookies help us understand how users interact with the site. You can learn more about cookies at allaboutcookies.org.


6. Your Data Protection Rights

Under GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Request access to your personal data.
  • Request correction or deletion of your data.
  • Object to the processing of your data in certain circumstances.

Since we do not store or process identifiable personal data beyond email correspondence, these rights are rarely applicable. However, feel free to contact us if you have concerns.


7. GDPR Compliance

Now Mindful acts as a data processor under legitimate interest grounds for site analytics and correspondence purposes. Analytics data is anonymized and does not include personally identifiable information (PII).


8. Contact Information

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at contact@nowmindful.co.uk.
