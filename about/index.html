About Now Mindful


Welcome to Now Mindful, where mindfulness meets the joy of play! We're here to make meditation accessible, playful, and something to look forward to—even if sitting still has never been your thing.


Our Mission

At Now Mindful, we believe mindfulness can be found in the present moment—especially when we're in a state of play. Our mission is to create playful, relaxed settings where mindfulness feels less like a chore and more like an adventure.

Whether through gamified meditation, technology projects, or engaging talks, we're here to spark the realization that mindfulness is available to everyone.


Our Story

Founded in 2022, Now Mindful began as an initiative to teach mantra meditation online. As we grew, we noticed how hard it can be to access meditation teachings in a world that values constant outward productivity. We knew it was time to take a different approach—one that makes mindfulness feel natural and approachable, no matter your starting point.

Our journey is deeply personal. Mindfulness became an anchor for our founder, David, during a challenging time as a carer for his sister. It helped ease the fear of death and unlocked a new perspective on life. That transformative experience shaped the heart of Now Mindful—helping others discover the freedom mindfulness can offer.


What We Offer

We bring mindfulness to life through:

  • Gamified Meditation: Making mindfulness interactive and fun.
  • Technology Development: Including an exciting video game project currently in the works!
  • Streams and Talks: Engaging video content and live discussions to share insights and techniques.

Our Values

Guided by simplified Buddhist principles, we value:

  • Kindness: Toward ourselves and others.
  • Mindfulness: Being present with what is.
  • Playfulness: Finding joy in the process.
  • Growth: Learning and evolving as we go.
  • Openness: Welcoming everyone, no matter where they are on their journey.

Who It's For

If sitting still makes you squirm, you're in the right place. We're here for anyone who's ever said, “Meditation's just not for me!” and anyone curious about mindfulness but unsure where to start. If you're ready to explore mindfulness in a way that fits your life, welcome aboard.


Why Choose Now Mindful?

Because mindfulness doesn't have to be intimidating or boring. We're here to prove it can be joyful, accessible, and yes—even playful.
